Welcome to Our Health Tracker!

This tool helps you gauge how far you are from your ideal macronutrient goals.

Getting Started: Two Simple Steps

1. Enter Your Food Items

Go to the search page and start adding foods. We source our detailed food entries from FDC API.

2. Provide Your Personal Information

We use your details (age, weight, height) to estimate personalized target goals for each macronutrient.
Note on Activity Level: The CDC uses four activity categories.
For simplicity, we currently assume all users are Active adults.
If gender isn't selected Male is assumed.
